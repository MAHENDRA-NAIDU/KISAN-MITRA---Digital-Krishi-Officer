Government Schemes for Farmers

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

An income support scheme where all eligible small and marginal farmer families get up to ₹6,000 per year.

Learn More & Apply

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

An insurance service for farmers for their yields. It provides comprehensive insurance cover against failure of the crop, thus helping to stabilise the income of farmers.

Learn More & Apply

Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme

A scheme that provides farmers with timely access to credit for their cultivation and other needs.
